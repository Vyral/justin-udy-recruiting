There are three things that brokers won’t tell you that I want to reveal today.

Schedule a 30 Min Sit Down Today

Over my years in the business, I’ve noticed that there are three things that your broker won’t tell you.

First of all, unfortunately, not all brokers are concerned with your success. They won’t tell you that they just want you to join their brokerage because they want that monthly fee.

Secondly, they don’t have the leads and systems that they promise you. Many past brokers say they have a lot of leads, and maybe they do have multiple qualified leads. Maybe they are getting 25 leads a month. However, not all brokers are focused on lead generation. They are focused on getting more agents.

“
Most brokers are focused on getting more agents, not lead generation.

”

What happens is they get 25 leads a month and end up hiring 75 people because they are focused on growing the brokerage. As an agent, you might only wind up with one lead a month. After all, what they focus on is what they get.

Finally, brokers won’t tell you that they don’t have the experience or training to help you become the best producer in the market. There are some amazing brokers who have acquired a lot of knowledge over the years. Unfortunately, they have never followed the path you want to take.

These brokers have never sold 100 or 200 homes a year or built systems to help you get the leverage you need to have a life of freedom. They may give you some nuggets of knowledge to help you sell an extra home a year, but you need to work with a true professional if you want to know how to handle volume, get support from established systems, and gain leverage.
